About Us

Crown Cupcakes is a friend owned bakery located in London, UK. Everything is made with passion and kindness. We strive to use the best ingredients in order to create only the best for our customers. We are Open daily and our cupcakes are baked fresh right on location every morning! Every month a new specialty cupcake is released named after a member of the Royal family.

The Team

Rick Alster

Rick is our head baker at Crown Cupcakes. Born and raised in the country side of England, Rick spend a lot of his childhood baking with his grandma. His favourite cupcake here is strawberries and cream.

Kyle Marter

Kyle is our main man on the business side at Crown Cupcakes. Resided in London England, Rick went to university and graduated with a Business degree. His favourite cupcake here is coffee and cocoa.

Suzy Cho

Suzy is our head decorator at Crown Cupcakes. Suzy attended a culinary school in Itay to learn from the best. Her favourite cupcake here is salted crunchy caramel.
